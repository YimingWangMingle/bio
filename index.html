Ph.D. Candidate @ UM

Yiming Wang 王一鸣

I am currently a Ph.D. Candidate in State Key Laboratory of Internet of Things for Smart City (University of Macau), fortunate to be advised by Prof. Leong Hou U, Ryan. I also had worked with Prof. Bo An , Prof. Steven Morad , Prof. Yali Du , Prof. Jiayu Chen and Prof. Yang Yu . Before joining UM, I obtained my bachelor's degree from the Department of Computing and Information Science at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in 2020. I am interested in improving the efficiacy and generalization of autonomous agents using simulation, reinforcement learning, and causal discovery.

I am passionate about pursuing research that is both practically impactful or scientifically interesting.
(Recruiting) Always open to collaborators.
